Why It's Free

NLA 25 is absolutely free to play! We believe everyone should have the chance to enjoy a realistic football experience, without spending a dollar. There's no need for Robux or any in-game currency to access the core gameplay.

However, if you're looking to enhance your experience, there will be in-game purchases available. But remember, these are completely optional, and you can still enjoy the full game for free!

Platform Availability

For now, NLA 25 will be available on PC and consoles. We are constantly working to improve and expand the game, and we’re considering bringing it to mobile devices soon.
